Accept FSA & HSA Cards — Open New Payment Options for Patients

One of the biggest advantages healthcare practices can offer is the ability to accept FSA (Flexible Spending Account) and HSA (Health Savings Account) cards. Many patients prefer using these pre-tax dollars for eligible medical expenses rather than paying out of pocket.

Our terminals accept FSA and HSA cards wherever Visa and Mastercard are accepted. This expands your patient payment options and can accelerate payment collection — patients are often eager to use their FSA/HSA balances before the plan year ends.

💳

FSA Card Support

Flexible Spending Account cards process just like regular debit cards on our Clover terminals. No special setup required. Patients can use pre-tax dollars for eligible procedures.

💰

HSA Card Support

Health Savings Account cards are widely used for medical expenses and work seamlessly on our systems. Patients appreciate the tax advantage, and it increases collection rates.

✅

Instant Authorization

FSA and HSA cards verify instantly at the point of sale. No complex verification steps. Process payments as you normally would with any card.

Payment Plans & Patient Financing Options

High-ticket procedures often require payment flexibility. Clover allows you to set up payment plans directly within the system, letting patients spread costs over multiple months while you collect installment payments automatically.

For larger treatment plans or cosmetic procedures, we can connect you with patient financing partners who offer promotional financing (0% APR options) to qualified patients. This removes price barriers and helps patients say yes to necessary or elective care.

Many practices use a combination approach: small payment plans run through Clover, larger procedures financed through third-party partners. We'll help you identify which strategy works best for your practice type.

Security & Compliance — Built for Healthcare

Clear fact: Payment card data is not covered by HIPAA. HIPAA protects health information (diagnoses, treatments, medical history). Credit card and payment data are handled under PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard), not HIPAA. We are fully PCI DSS compliant.

🔒

PCI DSS Level 1

We maintain the highest level of PCI compliance. Your patient payment data is protected by industry-leading security standards and encrypted end-to-end.

🛡

EMV & Tokenization

All transactions use EMV chip card technology and tokenization, which means card data is never stored on your devices. Fraudsters can't steal what isn't there.

🔐

Data Isolation

Payment data is completely separated from patient health records. No mixing of PCI and PHI data. We handle payments; your EHR system handles medical records.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept FSA and HSA cards?

Yes. Our terminals accept FSA (Flexible Spending Account) and HSA (Health Savings Account) cards wherever Visa and Mastercard are accepted. Patients can pay for eligible medical expenses with their FSA/HSA balance directly. This is one of the biggest advantages healthcare practices can offer.

Can cash discount work for a medical practice?

It depends on your practice type. For elective procedures (medical spa, cosmetic dentistry, veterinary, cosmetic surgery), cash discount works very well and patients appreciate the transparency. For insurance-billed services, you'll want to discuss specific implementation with us because dynamics differ. We'll give you an honest assessment of whether it makes sense for your practice.

Do you handle insurance billing or claims?

No. We're a payment processing company, not a medical billing company. We handle the credit card and debit card payments from patients — insurance billing and claims remain your responsibility or your billing service's responsibility. We keep payment processing separate from billing complexity.

Can I set up payment plans for patients?

Yes. Clover allows you to process installment payments manually, and we can connect you with patient financing partners for larger treatment plans. Many practices use a combination of processing and third-party financing. We'll help you structure the right approach for your practice type.

Is your processing HIPAA compliant?

Payment processing data is not subject to HIPAA (it doesn't include protected health information like diagnoses or medical history). However, we take security seriously — all our systems are PCI DSS compliant, use end-to-end encryption, and meet the highest industry security standards. Your patient payment data is protected.
