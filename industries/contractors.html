Cash Discount — Your Customers Prefer It Anyway

Here's a fact about contractors: your customers often want to pay cash or check. Cash discount programs work exceptionally well in the trades because it aligns with customer preference.

Here's how it works: You list your price. Customers who pay by card pay a small service fee (typically 3.99%). Customers who pay by check or cash pay your listed price. Simple. Transparent. And most of your customers won't mind because they already want to pay by check or cash.

The result: Lower effective processing costs, faster cash in hand, and happier customers who feel they're getting a fair deal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I accept payments at the job site without WiFi?

Yes. Our Clover Flex and Clover Go devices use 4G LTE cellular connectivity, so you can accept cards anywhere in NC — on a rooftop, in a crawlspace, or at a remote job site. No WiFi required. If you're in an area with weak cellular signal (rare), the device caches the transaction and processes it once signal returns.

How does cash discount work for large contractor invoices?

The same 3.99% service fee applies regardless of ticket size. On a $10,000 job, the service fee is $399 — shown clearly on the customer's receipt. Customers who pay by check or cash pay your listed price. Most contractor customers have no issue with this because many prefer paying by check anyway. It's transparent and fair.

Can I send digital invoices from my phone?

Yes. With Clover, you can send professional digital invoices via email or text directly from your phone or tablet, collect payments online, and track which invoices are paid vs. outstanding. No more chasing checks or wondering if an invoice got lost in someone's inbox. You can follow up on overdue invoices with a single tap.

How quickly do I receive funds after a job?

Funds deposit to your bank account as fast as the next business day. For a large job completed on Friday, you'll have the funds Monday morning. This fast cash flow is huge for contractors — you can pay your suppliers and crew faster, which strengthens relationships and improves your purchasing power.

Can I run multiple technicians on the same account?

Yes. You can add multiple employee profiles to your Clover account, giving each technician their own login while all transactions report to your central dashboard. This means you maintain full visibility into your business while empowering your team to process payments independently.
