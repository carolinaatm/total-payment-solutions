Common Questions

Carnival & Event Processing FAQ

Can I accept credit cards at a carnival without WiFi?

Yes. The Clover Flex and Clover Go operate over 4G/LTE cellular data, which means you don't need WiFi at the venue. Transactions process securely over the cellular network — even in fields, fairgrounds, and remote outdoor locations across NC, SC, and VA.

What if I only run events seasonally?

We offer flexible seasonal account options. You can pause service between events and reactivate when your season starts. There are no long-term contracts and no penalty for seasonal use — you pay only when you're actively processing.

How many terminals do I need for a large fair or carnival?

That depends on the number of revenue points — rides, games, food stands, ticketing, etc. We'll work with you to determine the right number of devices. Each Clover Flex or Go operates independently, so you can deploy as many as your event requires. Many large carnivals deploy 10–30 units.

Can I use the same terminals at multiple events?

Absolutely. Your Clover devices travel with you from show to show. As long as there is cellular signal, you can process transactions at any location — no re-setup required. One account, one support team, unlimited locations.

Does the cash discount program work for carnivals?

Yes — and it works exceptionally well for event environments. Customers expect some price variation at fairs and carnivals. Applying a 3.99% service fee to card transactions eliminates your processing costs, bringing your monthly bill to $100 or less regardless of how many transactions you process.
