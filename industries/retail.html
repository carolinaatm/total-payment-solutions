Retail Processing Questions

Will retail customers accept a service fee for card payments?

Retail is one of the easiest industries to implement cash discount. Most customers have seen the practice at gas stations and many retailers. Compliant signage at the register explains the pricing, and the vast majority of customers have no issue. You'll likely see a shift toward cash payments for smaller transactions, which reduces your processing costs even further.

Can I manage my inventory with the Clover POS?

Yes. Clover's inventory system lets you track stock levels, set low-stock alerts, categorize products, and generate inventory reports. You can also manage multiple locations from one dashboard. The system is designed for retail and integrates with suppliers, making reordering simple.

Does Clover support a customer loyalty program?

Yes. Clover has a built-in loyalty program that lets you reward repeat customers with points, discounts, or perks. You can run promotions, manage your customer database, and see which customers are your most valuable — all directly from the system.

Can I sell online and in-store with the same system?

Clover integrates with e-commerce platforms to keep your in-store and online inventory synced. This is a significant advantage for retailers who want to expand online without managing two separate systems. When you sell a product online, your in-store inventory updates automatically.

What if I have multiple retail locations?

We support multi-location retail businesses. Each location can have its own terminal while reporting flows into a centralized dashboard for your review. You'll see sales, inventory, and employee performance across all locations in one place.
