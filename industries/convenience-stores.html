The Problem

Processing Fees Are Killing C-Store Profits

Convenience stores run on thin margins — typically 1–3% net profit. Credit card processing fees of 2–4% per transaction can erase those margins entirely.

The Math on a $30,000/Month C-Store

At a typical 2.7% processing rate, you're paying $810/month — or $9,720/year — just to accept cards. With our cash discount program, that drops to $100/month or less.

Why C-Stores Get Hit Hardest

  • ✓High transaction volume — fees add up fast on hundreds of daily sales
  • ✓Low average ticket — percentage fees are disproportionately large on $5–$20 purchases
  • ✓Card usage is increasing — more customers paying with cards every year
  • ✓National processors charge c-stores premium rates — they know you have few alternatives

The Solution: Cash Discount Program

A 3.99% service fee is shown on the customer's receipt for card payments. Cash customers pay the listed price. You collect the same amount on every sale — processing fees go to zero.
