Tip Handling with Cash Discount

Tips are processed separately and are not subject to the service fee. Your clients add their tip after seeing the service fee on the screen — just like at a restaurant. The process is familiar and hasn't been an issue for any of our salon clients.

💰

Two-Step Payment Process

Client sees the service fee. Clover displays the amount and explains the fee. Client then adds their tip (if they choose) on top. Tips flow separately and are not subject to the service fee — only the service is.

✅

Client Acceptance

This process is familiar from restaurants and many other service businesses. Clients understand and expect it. Our salon clients report zero pushback on tips — if anything, tips often increase because the tip decision comes after the service experience is fresh.

Salon clients are used to tipping for services. The cash discount program applies to the service only. Tips are always optional and separate. This transparency actually increases client confidence in your pricing.

Salon Processing Questions

How does tip handling work with cash discount at a salon?

Tips are processed separately and are not subject to the service fee. Your clients add their tip after seeing the service fee on the screen, just like at a restaurant. The process is familiar and hasn't been an issue for any of our salon clients. If anything, tips often increase because the tip decision comes after the service experience is fresh.

Can I track retail product sales and services in the same system?

Yes. Clover handles both service tickets and retail product inventory in one system. You can track which stylists are selling retail, run promotions, and manage stock levels — all in one place. No separate inventory system. No manual tracking. Just one dashboard.

Do you work with booth renters and independent stylists?

We do. We can set up individual processing accounts for booth renters so each stylist manages their own payments independently. We can also set up a single salon account with multiple employee profiles, so stylists have individual logins but all payments flow to the salon owner. Choose the model that fits your business.

Does Clover integrate with salon booking software?

Clover integrates with several popular booking platforms through the App Market, including options compatible with Vagaro, Booksy, and similar platforms. We'll confirm compatibility with your specific software during setup to ensure seamless sync between your appointments and payments.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contracts. We operate month-to-month. If you decide to leave for any reason, there's no termination fee. We earn your business every month. This applies to both payment processing and Clover POS.
