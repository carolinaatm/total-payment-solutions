Frequently Asked Questions

Will restaurant customers accept a service fee for card payments?

Yes, and more than you'd expect. Most diners understand the model — it's similar to how service fees appear on restaurant bills already. The fee shows clearly on the receipt. Very few customers object, and those who do can always pay cash. In fact, guests appreciate the transparency. They know exactly what they're paying and why, and many respect your honesty about covering your processing costs.

Can the cash discount program work with tableside ordering?

Absolutely. Our Clover Flex terminals handle tableside ordering, payment, and tip adjustment all in one device. The service fee is automatically calculated and shown to the guest before they sign. It becomes part of the normal transaction flow — no confusion, no surprises. Guests see the 3.99% fee, accept it, and complete their payment right at the table.

How does tip adjustment work with the cash discount program?

Tips are handled separately and are not subject to the service fee. Guests add their tip after the service fee is displayed, keeping the tip process straightforward and familiar. This is important because your staff continues to receive full tip income without any reduction. The service fee covers your processing costs; tips go to your team in full.

Can I integrate with my existing restaurant management software?

Clover integrates with most major restaurant management platforms including 7Shifts, Toast (limited), OpenTable, Yelp Waitlist, and more through the Clover App Market. If you use a platform not listed, we can discuss workarounds or alternative integration approaches. Our team will help you find a solution that works with your existing systems.

Do you work with bars and nightclubs?

Yes. We have specific experience with bar and nightclub environments including bar tab management, bartender-controlled terminals, and end-of-night settlement features. Our Clover Go terminals are perfect for high-volume bar service. We understand the unique operational challenges of bars and have solutions tailored for that environment.
