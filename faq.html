Cash Discount & Fees

A cash discount program allows you to pass your credit card processing fees on to customers who pay by card, while offering a discount to customers who pay cash. Customers paying by card pay a small service fee (typically 3.99%), while your monthly processing bill drops to $100 or less — guaranteed.

Yes. Cash discount programs are legal in all 50 states and are fully compliant with Visa, Mastercard, and Amex rules when implemented correctly. We handle the compliant setup for you.

When the majority of your transactions are card-based, the service fee collected from card-paying customers covers your processing costs. The result: your net processing expense is $100/month or less, regardless of volume.

Most don't. The fee (typically 3.99%) is clearly disclosed at the terminal and on the receipt. Customers who prefer to avoid it can pay cash and receive a discount. In practice, the vast majority of businesses see no meaningful drop in card volume after switching.

We guarantee your net processing cost will be $100/month or less. If it's ever more, we'll credit the difference.
