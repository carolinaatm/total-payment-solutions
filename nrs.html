NRS POS — Frequently Asked Questions

What is NRS?

NRS (National Retail Solutions) is a point-of-sale system built specifically for convenience stores, tobacco shops, bodegas, and high-volume independent retailers. It includes age verification, EBT/SNAP, lottery management, and inventory systems for thousands of SKUs. NRS Pay provides the payment processing, or you can pair it with Total Payment Solutions for zero-fee processing.

Does NRS include payment processing?

Yes. NRS includes NRS Pay for payment processing. But we recommend pairing NRS hardware with Total Payment Solutions' cash discount program — it will lower your processing costs to $100/month or less, often zero.

Can I get zero processing fees with NRS?

Yes. With our cash discount program, a 3.99% service fee is added to card transactions. Cash customers pay the listed price. Your cost goes to zero. Combined with NRS hardware, it's the best option for convenience stores and high-volume retail.

Does NRS have age verification for tobacco and alcohol?

Yes. NRS has built-in ID scanning, age verification, and compliance tracking for tobacco and alcohol sales. Automated ID reading, failed verification logs, and state-mandated reporting are all included.

Does NRS accept EBT and SNAP?

Yes. Full EBT and SNAP support is built-in. Split transactions, separate tracking, and state compliance are included — no additional setup or fees.
