Why Richmond Businesses Choose Local Merchant Services

Serving Richmond's diverse and growing business community since 2014.

From restaurants on Cary Street to retail in Short Pump, convenience stores along Broad Street to contractors serving the growing suburbs — Total Payment Solutions helps Richmond businesses eliminate processing fees.
