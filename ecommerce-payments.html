E-Commerce Payment FAQs

What e-commerce platforms do you integrate with?

We integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Wix, Squarespace, and most major platforms. We also offer a hosted payment page and API access for custom-built stores.

Can I use the cash discount program for online sales?

Yes. Our service fee program can be applied to online transactions. A clearly disclosed service fee is added at checkout for card payments. Cash discount programs are legal in all 50 states.

What is a virtual terminal?

A virtual terminal lets you accept card payments over the phone by manually entering card details into a secure web portal — no physical terminal required. Ideal for businesses that take phone orders.

How are chargebacks handled?

Our team assists with chargeback documentation and disputes. We provide guidance on best practices to reduce chargeback risk, including receipt requirements, refund policies, and fraud tools.
