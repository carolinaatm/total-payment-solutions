Frequently Asked Questions

What is interchange-plus pricing?

Interchange-plus means you pay the actual interchange rate set by Visa/Mastercard plus a small fixed markup from TPS. This is more transparent than flat-rate or tiered pricing and usually results in lower overall costs for most businesses. With interchange-plus, there are no surprises — you see exactly what the card networks charge and exactly what we add on top.

How does your cash discount program work?

With our cash discount program, a 3.99% service fee is added to card transactions and shown on the customer's receipt. Cash customers pay the listed price. Your processing cost goes to zero — $100/month or less, guaranteed. It's legal in all 50 states and most customers understand and accept the model, especially in retail, hospitality, and convenience store environments.

Is there a contract or termination fee?

No. We offer month-to-month service with no early termination fees. We earn your business every month by delivering great service and fair pricing, not by locking you into contracts you can't escape.

How fast do I receive my funds?

Most businesses receive funds the next business day. Depending on your bank and settlement time, same-day funding may also be available. Next-day funding keeps your cash flow moving and means you have access to your money when you need it.

Can I keep my existing equipment?

In many cases, yes. We can reprogram compatible terminals to work with our processing. If you need new equipment, we can often provide it at no cost as part of our program. We'll evaluate what you have and give you honest advice about what makes sense.
