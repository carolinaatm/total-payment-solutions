Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ATM placement.

How much does ATM placement cost my business?

Zero. We provide the machine, installation, and maintenance at no cost. You simply provide space and a power outlet. We own and operate the ATM — you just collect the surcharge revenue it generates.

How does the surcharge revenue work?

When a customer uses your ATM, they pay a surcharge fee (typically $2.50–$3.00 per transaction). That fee is split between Total Payment Solutions and your business. Most of our locations earn $200–$600/month, depending on foot traffic and transaction volume. You'll receive your portion via monthly check or ACH deposit.

Who loads the cash into the ATM?

Total Payment Solutions handles all cash loading and replenishment. We load the initial cash during installation and refill the machine as needed based on usage patterns. You never need to touch the machine, manage cash logistics, or worry about security concerns.

What are the location requirements?

We need a dedicated power outlet, adequate foot traffic (we'll evaluate your location), and compliance with local regulations. Most businesses qualify easily. Common locations include convenience stores, gas stations, restaurants, bars, hotels, laundromats, and office buildings. We'll assess your specific location and let you know within 24 hours.

How long does installation take?

Typically 1–2 hours. We schedule installation at a time that's convenient for your business — early morning, after hours, or during a slow period. Our team handles all setup, programming, connection testing, and cash loading. You'll be up and running and earning revenue the same day.
