Common Questions

Cash Discount Program FAQ

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is the cash discount program legal in North Carolina?

Yes. Cash discount programs are legal in all 50 states. Federal law permits merchants to offer a discount to customers who pay with cash versus credit card. The key requirement is proper disclosure at the point of sale — which our compliant terminals and signage handle automatically.

Will my customers be upset about the service fee?

Most customers understand and accept it — similar to how gas stations have displayed cash vs. credit prices for decades. Our terminal clearly displays both prices, and the vast majority of businesses using our program report very little pushback. Customers who prefer cash simply pay the lower price.

What equipment do I need?

We provide a free Clover terminal that is pre-programmed for the cash discount program. No existing equipment is required. We deliver it, set it up, and train your staff — all at no cost to you.

How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are up and running within 3–5 business days of approval. We handle all setup, programming, signage, and delivery. Your staff simply uses the terminal as instructed.

What if I process under $5,000 per month?

We work with businesses of all sizes. Even lower-volume merchants benefit from the program — you still eliminate processing fees and pay a flat $100 or less per month. We'll review your specific situation and give you an honest recommendation based on your volume and business type.

Is the 3.99% fee the same for all card types?

Yes. The service fee is applied consistently across all card types — Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and Amex. There is no difference in the fee based on the card used, which simplifies your pricing and keeps the program fully compliant.
