Who We Are

Our Story

Total Payment Solutions was founded in 2014 as an ATM placement and servicing company serving the Carolinas. From day one, we saw firsthand how much national credit card processors were overcharging local NC businesses — often 50% more than they should have been paying.

That insight drove us to expand from ATM services into full merchant services. We built a simple mission: give local businesses the same transparent pricing and real support that big corporations take for granted. Today, we serve 500+ merchants across North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia.

We're not a call center. We're a North Carolina company that answers the phone, knows your business by name, and shows up when you need us.
